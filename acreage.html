Beautiful Acreage Properties

Acreage properties offer expansive land, perfect for those seeking a rural lifestyle with ample space. Enjoy the tranquility of nature, the beauty of wide-open spaces, and the freedom to create your own paradise.

Features include large plots of land, potential for farming or gardening, and breathtaking views. Ideal for families looking to escape the city and enjoy a peaceful environment.

Places that are best for buying Acreages

Samford Valley: With rolling hills, thick woods, and picturesque views, Samford Valley is a tranquil rural area north-west of Brisbane's central business district. Samford Valley acreage estates provide plenty of room for hobby farming, horse paddocks, or just relaxing in the peace and quiet of nature. The region is renowned for its thriving local markets and community as well.

Brookfield: Known for its vast acreage estates, immaculate wilderness, and equestrian amenities, Brookfield is located south-west of the CBD. Brookfield's acreage homes provide a peaceful country escape while yet being close to all of Brisbane's conveniences, including schools.

Pullenvale: Located in Brisbane's western suburbs, Pullenvale offers spacious lots that are encircled by animal corridors and native vegetation. Pullenvale's acreage homes provide solitude and quiet, making them a great option for anyone looking for a tranquil country lifestyle that is yet accessible to the city.

Upper Brookfield: Adjacent to Brookfield, Upper Brookfield boasts huge acreage estates and expansive vistas of the surrounding countryside, contributing to a similar rustic atmosphere. Upper Brookfield acreage homes provide chances for hiking, horseback riding, and taking in the diverse range of local plants and animals.

Chandler: Known for its expansive rural properties and equestrian amenities, Chandler is a city outside of Brisbane's south-east suburbs. Chandler acreage estates provide a tranquil haven from the hustle and bustle of the city, all while being close to local services and important transportation hubs.
