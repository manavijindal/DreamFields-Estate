Stylish Duplex Homes

Duplex homes offer the perfect blend of comfort and privacy. With two separate living spaces in one property, these homes are ideal for extended families or those looking to rent out part of their home.

Features include modern design, private gardens, and the flexibility of dual living. Enjoy the benefits of home ownership with additional rental income potential.

Places that are best for buying Duplex

Paddington: Offering a blend of historic and modern duplex apartments, Paddington is renowned for its dynamic environment and attractive character homes. Paddington is popular with both investors and those looking for a modern lifestyle because of its close proximity to the CBD, hip cafes, quaint stores, and green streets.

Woolloongabba: Located just south of the CBD, Woolloongabba is a desirable region for duplex investment because to its fast urban regeneration. Woolloongabba provides convenience and capital growth possibilities because to its near proximity to major hospitals, universities, and athletic events.

Greenslopes: This well-liked area located south-east of the central business district and is renowned for its lush lanes and welcoming community atmosphere for families. In Greenslopes, duplex homes frequently provide roomy floor plans, backyard areas, and convenient access to neighbourhood parks, schools, and public transit.

Stafford: Situated in the northern suburbs of Brisbane, Stafford provides reasonably priced duplex alternatives in a handy location. Stafford attracts to first-time homebuyers as well as investors looking for rental possibilities because of its close proximity to important shopping centres, educational institutions, and recreational amenities.

Nundah: Located in the inner north of Brisbane, Nundah has seen a lot of gentrification recently, which has raised demand for duplex homes. Nundah provides a desired urban lifestyle at a more reasonable price range with its boutique stores, hip cafés, and convenient access to public transit.
