Luxurious houses

Houses provide the ultimate in luxury and exclusivity. Located in prime locations, these homes offer spacious interiors, private pools, and beautifully landscaped gardens.

Features include high-end finishes, ample living space, and top-of-the-line amenities. Ideal for those seeking a luxurious lifestyle with privacy and comfort.

Places that are best for buying Villa style Houses

Ascot: Known for its large, artistically planned mansions and tree-lined avenues, Ascot is a wealthy neighbourhood in Brisbane's inner north. Ascot villas are known for their opulent interiors, large grounds, and swimming pools. Ascot's esteemed status makes it appealing to individuals looking for a posh neighbourhood with quick access to renowned schools, great eating, and boutique shopping.

Hamilton: Hamilton, which is next door to Ascot, has equally opulent villa-style homes along the Brisbane River. Hamilton provides the perfect combination of luxury and tranquilly with its riverside setting and expansive views of the cityscape. Large outdoor gathering spaces, private pontoons, and access to upscale facilities like marinas and golf courses are common features of villa rentals in Hamilton.

Bulimba: Known for its village feel and quaint heritage homes, Bulimba is a sought-after neighbourhood located on the eastern banks of the Brisbane River. Bulimba's villa-style homes combine contemporary luxury with traditional elegance, and many of them have high-end finishes, manicured gardens, and views of the river. Bulimba is a popular choice for people looking for a laid-back yet sophisticated lifestyle because of its lively dining areas, upscale stores, and riverside parks.

Chelmer: An upmarket riverbank district south-west of the CBD, Chelmer is distinguished by its tree-lined lanes and old-fashioned residences. Chelmer's villa-style homes are known for their classic elegance and sophistication. Many of these homes have large verandas, well-kept gardens, and private river access. Chelmer provides a peaceful haven near to the city, with its tight-knit community feel and close access to top schools and parklands.

Paddington: Located to the west of the central business district, Paddington is well-known for its unique blend of Victorian-era residences, upscale stores, and hip cafés. Paddington villas combine traditional architectural elements with contemporary amenities, providing roomy living areas, outdoor eating areas, and expansive city views. Paddington is a desirable neighbourhood for individuals looking for a lush suburban environment with a bustling cultural scene close to the central business district.
