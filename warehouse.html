Spacious Warehouses

Warehouses offer ample storage space and are equipped with the latest facilities to ensure smooth operations. Located in industrial areas with easy access to transportation routes, they are perfect for logistics and storage needs.

Features include large storage areas, loading docks, and secure environments.

Places that are best for buying Warehouses

Port of Brisbane: This important centre for logistics and transportation is situated at the mouth of the Brisbane River. This region is great for firms engaged in import/export, distribution, and logistics since it is accessible to major roads, shipping terminals, and rail links.

Rocklea: One of Brisbane's main industrial districts, Rocklea is located south of the city centre. Large industrial estates and superior transportation connections via Beaudesert Road and the Ipswich Motorway make Rocklea a great place for distribution and storage businesses. The Brisbane Markets, a hub for the delivery of fresh goods, are also located in this region.

Acacia Ridge: Another well-known industrial area close to Rocklea, Acacia Ridge is home to several manufacturing and storage facilities. The region is well-served by important highways like the Logan and Ipswich motorways, and also has a significant rail freight terminal, which makes it an ideal site for distribution and logistics hubs.

Hemmant: A major industrial neighbourhood with plenty of warehouse space, Hemmant is located close to the Port of Brisbane. Because of its advantageous position, which makes it simple to get to the port, Brisbane Airport, and the Gateway Motorway, it is a great place for businesses that deal with manufacturing, distribution, and logistics.

Pinkenba: Situated near the Port of Brisbane and the Brisbane Airport, Pinkenba is a highly desirable site for warehouses that need to be handy to both air and marine freight services. The region is ideal for a variety of sectors since it is well-served by the Gateway Motorway and has a large amount of industrial and storage facilities.
